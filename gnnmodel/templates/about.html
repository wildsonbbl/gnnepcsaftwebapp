GNNPCSAFT Web App

GNNPCSAFT combines the use of Graph Neural Networks to estimate pure-component parameters for the PC-SAFT equation of state, making parameter estimation faster and more accessible.

Open Source AI-Powered Cross-Platform

How does it work?
Just provide a SMILES or InChI string, and GNNPCSAFT will estimate the PC-SAFT parameters for your molecules.

Additionally, you can test the estimated parameters on some properties, like density, vapor pressure, enthalpy of vaporization, phase diagrams, VLE, LLE, etc.

Tip: Find molecular identifiers at PubChem or build your own at DIYmol (right-click the 2D panel to copy SMILES).
