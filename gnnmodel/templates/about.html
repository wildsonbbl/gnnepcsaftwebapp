GNNePCSAFT

This app uses a GNNePCSAFT model to estimate ePC-SAFT parameters. Parameters can be used with FEOS package, DWSIM or any other implementation of the EoS.

You can find InChI and SMILES at pubchem or you can build your own molecule at DIYmol and copy the SMILES right clicking on the 2D panel.
