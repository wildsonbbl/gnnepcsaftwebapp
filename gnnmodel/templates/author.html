I'm a Chemical Engineer, MSc and Data scientist with over 6 years of research experience. I started my career as an undergraduate researcher at the Biochemical Engineering Laboratory from UFRN/BRAZIL in 2017 while I was a Chemical Engineer Bachelor student. Since then, I've been part of many biotechnology research projects, such as lactase/ethanol co-production and enzyme purification with deep eutectic solvents, which was the subject of my undergraduate thesis.

Approaching the end of 2019, I started developing expertise in Machine Learning and Data Science. In 2021 I completed my bachelor degree and started working toward a Chemical Engineering MSc degree also at UFRN/BRAZIL. GNNePCSAFT was my MSc project. The goal was to be able to advance research on complex species, such as deep eutectic solvents and ionic liquids, which is slowed down by the need of costly experimental work. GNNePCSAFT provides parameters for the ePC-SAFT EoS, thus allowing to predict thermodynamic properties with relative good accuracy. In this manner, those species can be extensively and throughly studied with process simulators and any other platform that implements the EoS.
