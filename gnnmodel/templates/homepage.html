GNNePCSAFT is a project focused in the use of graph neural networks to estimate pure-component parameters of the Equation of State ePC-SAFT. The motivation of this work is to be able to use a robust Equation of State without prior need of experimental data. Equations of State are important to calculate thermodynamic properties, and are pre-requisite in process simulators.

Currently, the model takes in account only the hard-chain and dispersive terms of ePC-SAFT. Future work on associative, polar and ionic terms are being considered. Code is being developed mainly in Pytorch (PYG). The model was trained with Ramírez-Vélez et al. (2022) parametrization data. ThermoML Archive experimental data is used to test the accuracy of the model predictions.
